We are making it faster to find the company you are looking for in the Codat Portal by promoting search to a permanent toolbar control on the Companies and Subsidiaries pages.

<!--truncate-->

## What's new?

On **May 6, 2026**, we are updating the [Codat Portal](https://app.codat.io) so that the search input is always visible on the Companies and Subsidiaries pages, instead of being hidden behind a search icon. Search now lives in a dedicated table toolbar directly above the table, alongside the connection status filter.

![Always-visible search on the Companies page](/img/updates/260506-always-visible-search.gif)

We are introducing the following improvements:

- The search input is **always visible** in a new table toolbar on the Companies and Subsidiaries pages — no more clicking a search icon to reveal it.
- The toolbar groups search and the connection-status filter together below the page header, while the **Invite** and **Create** actions remain in the page header.
- Focusing the empty search input opens a popover that lists exactly which fields you can search by: **Company ID**, **Company name**, **Connection ID**, **Connected platform**, and **User who created it**.
- An updated placeholder ("Search company, platform, ID...") makes the supported search terms clearer at a glance.

These improvements apply to the Companies and Subsidiaries pages and build on the [multi-entity company table](/blog/multi-entity-portal-update) we shipped earlier this year.

## Who is this relevant for?

This update benefits all customers who use the Codat Portal to manage their companies, and is especially useful for implementation, support, and customer-facing teams who frequently search across large numbers of companies and connections.

## How to get started?

No action is required to benefit from this update. Open the **Companies** or **Subsidiaries** page in the [Codat Portal](https://app.codat.io) from **May 6, 2026** and start typing in the search input at the top of the table.
